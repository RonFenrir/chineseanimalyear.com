Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Chinese zodiac fortune readings.

How accurate are the Chinese zodiac readings?

Our readings combine traditional Chinese zodiac wisdom with advanced astrological calculations to provide highly accurate insights. The Chinese zodiac system has been refined over thousands of years, and our algorithm enhances this ancient wisdom with modern analytical techniques.

How is my fortune calculated?

Your fortune is calculated using a proprietary algorithm that analyzes your birth date in relation to the Chinese lunar calendar, your zodiac sign's elemental properties, I Ching hexagrams, and Western astrological positions. We examine the interplay between these factors to generate a comprehensive and personalized reading.

What makes your readings different from others?

Our readings are unique because they bridge Eastern and Western mystical traditions. We incorporate Chinese zodiac wisdom, Feng Shui principles, I Ching divination, and Western astrological elements. This comprehensive approach provides more nuanced and actionable insights than single-tradition readings.

Can I get a reading for someone else?

Yes, you can generate a reading for anyone by entering their name and birth date. However, for the most accurate results, we recommend that individuals complete their own readings, as personal energy and intention can influence the interpretation.

What's included in the premium readings?

Premium readings include a more detailed personality analysis, comprehensive compatibility guide with all zodiac signs, career and wealth insights, and an annual fortune forecast. The Ultimate Reading adds monthly detailed forecasts, a personalized Feng Shui guide, I Ching hexagram analysis, and email support.

How often should I get a new reading?

While your zodiac sign and its core traits remain constant, the influence of cosmic energies changes throughout the year. We recommend refreshing your reading annually, ideally around the Lunar New Year, to stay aligned with the current celestial influences.
